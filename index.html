About Us


Moribo Creations Co-operative Limited it’s a company that endorse the value interest of fashion and design between the community through its integrated programmes, projects and products, design is currently taken as a career that requires high understanding of sewing machinery and is meant for the ones on skirts, our main focus is to change that discernment. The fact is that Fashion and Design are in the present age, omnipresent. Today possibly as a result of the eye-opening influence of trending clothing brands , people have started to that there is in fact Fashion and Design. Our brand expression encapsulates our stand on innovation tools regarding fashion and design for a better tomorrow, which is Carrying Your Own Cross.





Mission Statement

Moribo Creations Co Operative Limited mission is to work to develop the quality of life through fashion and design innovations and provide opportunities for all to share in the world of tomorrow. Moribo Creations Co-Operative Limited is all about tailoring to individual style at affordable market related pricing. Producing quality yet stylish fabrics, tailored to the customer’s individual needs.


Vision Statement

Moribo Creation Co-Operative Limited vision is to be a standard establishment in harnessing innovation for trend, design, social and youth expansion.
